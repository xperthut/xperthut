I have completed my Ph.D. from Washington State University, USA in August, 2020. I inaugurated here at Fall, 2015. My reseach focused on subgraph mining and visual analytics. During my research, I developed an exploratory data analysis tool named HYPPO–X that explore the high order structure of data in a form of graph. This graph is a topological representation of the data. I also developed algorithms on structural graph properties (path and flare) to identify divergent subpopulation besides resilient subpopulation.
